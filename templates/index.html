About Gentective

Gentective is a music genre classification system powered by advanced machine learning algorithms.

Our system analyzes various audio features including spectral features, rhythm patterns, and timbral textures to accurately predict the genre of your music.

Built with Python, Flask, and state-of-the-art machine learning models, Gentective provides fast and accurate genre classification for music enthusiasts and professionals alike.
